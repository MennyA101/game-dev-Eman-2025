EasyEDA YouTube Experience

My EasyEDA YouTube Experience

I followed this video tutorial to learn about EasyEDA: EasyEDA Tutorial Video on YouTube

What I Learned From the Video

The video introduced me to EasyEDA, a web-based tool for designing and simulating circuits. I learned how to:

  • Create a schematic diagram with electronic components.
  • Connect components together to make a working circuit.
  • Switch from schematic view to PCB layout view.
  • Design the physical layout of a circuit board (PCB).
  • Prepare the project to be manufactured by JLCPCB.

Watching the tutorial gave me hands-on knowledge of how electronic projects go from an idea → schematic → PCB → real product.

About JLCPCB

JLCPCB is a major PCB manufacturer. It works directly with EasyEDA, so after finishing my design in the video, I could see how simple it is to order the board online. JLCPCB is known for low-cost and high-quality PCB production.

About EasyEDA

EasyEDA is a free online circuit design tool. The video showed me how convenient it is because everything is in one place: schematic capture, simulation, PCB layout, and ordering. This makes it easier for beginners and professionals to design electronics.

RAKWIRELESS RAK3112 Module

Mr. Ellis also introduced the RAKWIRELESS RAK3112 module. It is a LoRaWAN communication device used in IoT (Internet of Things) projects. The module allows long-distance, low-power wireless communication. Applications include smart farming, smart cities, and environmental monitoring.
